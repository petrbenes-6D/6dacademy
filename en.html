About

Who is
Petr Beneš?

I am the founder of 6D Services — a Czech consultancy operating at the intersection of business strategy, AI/technology, and leadership development.

With over 20 years of experience in international banking and technology — including roles as CIO at GE Money and COO at Česká spořitelna — I bring real-world perspective to every engagement.

I work with executives, leadership teams and organizations across Central Europe on strategic transformation, AI adoption, and the human side of change.
